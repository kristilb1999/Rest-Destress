How to relax quickly

When we relax, the flow of blood
increases around our body giving us
more energy. It helps us to have a
calmer and clearer mind which aids
positive thinking, concentration,
memory and decision making. Relaxation
slows our heart rate, reduces our blood
pressure and relieves tension.
